# Ecommerce Sales & Customer Insight Analysis (Interactive Dashboard Using Excel)
## Project Objectives
The primary objectives of this analysis were to monitor overall sales and profitability performance, identify top revenue generating product categories, evaluate customer segment contributions, measure marketing channel effectiveness, analyze product return patterns and support strategic decision making through data visualization.

## Dataset Used:
-<a href="https://github.com/EricaAgboghidi/Excel-Ecommerce-Sales-Dashboard/blob/main/ecommerce_orders_dataset%20-%20Copy.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## KPI Questions
This dashboard was developed to provide a consolidated view of business performance and answer critical business questions like;
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which categories are the most profitable?
- How does revenue change over time?
- Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
- Which traffic sources drive the highest sales?
- Which countries generate the most revenue?
- Which categories have the highest return rates?
- Which brands generate the least revenue?

Before building the dashboard, the following data preparation steps were performed:
- Checked for missing values
- Verified data consistency
- Standardized categorical fields
- Removed duplicate records where necessary
- Created additional fields including: Return Flag, used to calculate return rates.

## Key Findings
- The Electronics category generated the highest revenue, contributing a significant portion of total sales, suggesting opportunities to review pricing and discount strategies.
- The Fashion category delivered the highest profit margin, indicating strong profitability despite generating less revenue than Electronics. 
- Returning customers contributed the largest share of total revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention and loyalty programs. 
- Social media emerged as the highest-performing traffic source in terms of revenue generation and some paid marketing channels generated lower returns, indicating opportunities to optimize marketing spend. 
- Saudi Arabia, United States and Canada accounted for the highest revenue among all countries. 
- The Fashion category recorded the highest return rate among all product categories.   

## Recommendations
- Focus on High-Profit Categories; increase marketing and inventory and prioritize products that contribute most to overall profitability. 
- Improve Underperforming Categories, review pricing, discounts, and supplier costs for categories with high sales but low profits. 
- Strengthen Customer Retention, expand loyalty and membership programs targeted at high-value customers. Implement personalized marketing campaigns to encourage repeat purchases. 
- Optimize Marketing Spend, reassess investment in lower-performing acquisition channels. 
- Reduce Product Returns. Improve product descriptions, images, and sizing information where applicable, monitor customer feedback to identify recurring product issues. 
- Expand Successful Markets. Replicate successful sales strategies from top-performing countries in emerging markets. Explore targeted marketing campaigns in regions with growth potential. 
- Leverage Seasonal Trends. Increase inventory and promotional activities ahead of peak sales periods. Use historical sales patterns to improve demand forecasting and stock management. 
## Conclusion
This analysis revealed that while revenue growth is primarily driven by a few high-performing product categories and customer segments, opportunities exist to improve profitability, reduce returns, and optimize marketing investments. By focusing on customer retention, product quality improvements, and data-driven marketing strategies, the business can enhance both customer satisfaction and long-term profitability.
